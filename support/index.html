What file formats are supported?

The PDF file format is the only supported format. To convert a Keynote slideshow into a PDF please follow the instructions on Apple's Support site. To convert a PowerPoint slideshow into a PDF please follow the instructions on Microsoft's Support site.

How do I load presentations?

Load presentations using iTunes USB File Sharing or from other apps using the "Open in..." menu.

Back to top

Why do I get an error when transferring a presentation to iTunes?

iTunes has a filename limitation that restricts it to files whose file names are 256 characters or less. Please rename presentation with long filenames before transferring via iTunes.

How do I view my presentations with a projector?

View your presentations on a projector by mirroring your iPhone via Airplay or with a Lightning adapter.

  • Apple TV
  • Apple Lightning to VGA Adapter
  • Apple Lightning to HDMI Adapter
  • Reflector
  • Air Server

How do I control presentations?

Change slides by pressing the top or bottom button on the right side of the Pebble. Swipe left-to-right on the iPhone to go back one slide. Swipe right-to-left or single tap on the iPhone to go forward one slide.

How do I exit presentations?

To exit a presentation long tap on the iPhone while viewing a presentation.

Back to top

How do I disable notifications while presenting?

Before presenting turn off Wi-Fi and Cellular Data in Settings to avoid receiving notifications. Do not turn on Airplane Mode as that will also disable Bluetooth which is required for wireless control. Do Not Disturb mode will not work as it still allows notifications when the phone is unlocked.

My presentations are displayed with a black background, how do I fix it?

When generating a PDF make sure a background color is set. When the background color is transparant most presentation software will try to be helpful and show your slides on top of a white background. To set a background color in your PowerPoint slides please follow the instructions on Microsoft's Support site.

What file formats are supported?

Currently, Google Drive presentations, PDF, and raw image formats are supported.

PDF presentations work best with widescreen (16:9) aspect ratio slides since the viewer shows the edge of the previous and next slides in standard (4:3) aspect ratio. Also, note that the app will show any PDF found in Google Drive, not just presentations. Very large PDFs may take quite awhile to load.

Back to top

How do I load presentations?

The app connects to your Google Drive account(s) and displays all of your Google Drive presentations, PDFs, and raw image presentations.

Back to top

How do I enable raw image presentations?

Choose the "Enable image presentations" menu option for each Drive account. This will create a "Wrist Presenter" folder. Create a subfolder for each raw image presentation and upload the images into it. The folder name will be used for the presentation name and images will be ordered alphabetically based on their file names.

How do I view my presentations with a projector?

View your presentations on a projector with a Chromecast, a Miracast dongle, or an HDMI adapter on compatible devices.

Can I view my presentations without a network connection?

When offline, any previously opened PDF and raw image presentations will be available. This makes it possible to use your presentations at conferences where network connectivity is often poor to nonexistent.
